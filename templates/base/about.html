About Us

The Dara knot is a symbol of unity and strength, originating from ancient Irish culture. In ancient Ireland, the Dara knot was used as a symbol of protection and was believed to bring good luck to those who wore it. The word is derived from the Old Irish word "dair" which also means oak tree.

The dara knot is also a nautical term for a type of knot used for securing gear during travel. The team behind this service is a group of fellow students from Code Institute.
We all share the experience of quality of our travels as a result of well thought out planning.
That is the reason we came up with the dara planner tool.

The development team for this project includes
Carmen Cantudo, Ian Buttimer, Nicklas Tanskanen and Dennis Jensen.
